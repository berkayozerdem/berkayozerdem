Ege AI - Aegean Region Artificial Intelligence Guide

My University Graduation Project

The Ege AI project aims to showcase the unique beauty, history, and stories of the Aegean region through a digital experience that touches the hearts of its users. Here are the highlights of the project:

Why Artificial Intelligence and LLM?

  • Modern users desire quick and personalized access to information. Leveraging LLM (Large Language Models), Ege AI provides context-aware responses, mythological storytelling, and personalized recommendations.
  • Using GPT-3.5, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), and other cutting-edge AI technologies, we aim to deliver an interactive and creative user experience.

Technologies Used

  • Data Processing: PyPDFLoader, TextSplitter, ChromaDB
  • Response Generation: GPT-3.5, RAG
  • Platform Development: React Native, LangChain

Project Features

  • Chatbot: An AI-powered chatbot at the heart of the project, answering Aegean-related queries on both web and mobile platforms.
  • Website: Showcasing stories, mythological narratives, and travel routes from the Aegean region.
  • Mobile App: Currently under development using React Native to ensure accessibility anytime and anywhere.
  • Social Media: Sharing Ege AI stories, travel tips, and regional beauty on Instagram and LinkedIn.

This project is a blend of technology and culture, reflecting the unique essence of the Aegean region while utilizing modern tools for an engaging user experience.
